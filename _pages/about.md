Associate Professor of Marketing (without tenure) at HEC Paris. Hi! PARIS Internal Chair Fellow. CESifo Research Network Affiliate.

I work at the intersection of industrial organization, quantitative marketing, and digitization. My research focuses on platform design, online reputation systems, competition policy, and the economic effects of artificial intelligence in digital markets.

I am one of the organizers of the <a href="https://www.digitaleconomics-paris.fr/conference-2025">Paris Digital Economics Conference</a>, an annual event that brings together researchers on the economics of digital platforms and technology markets.
